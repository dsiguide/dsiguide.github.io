    HiyaCFW (Flipnotehax)

    If you need help, ask the Nintendo Homebrew Discord!.
    I worked hard on this guide! If you want, you can donate through PayPal or Bitcoin!

    Make sure to follow the guide closely to ensure that you do everything correctly.

    Make sure to download the right files before proceeding with the guide.

    What you need

    • The latest release of Flipnotehax
    • An SD Card (must have at least 128mb of space)
    • Flipnote Studio for Nintendo DSi
    • The latest release of RocketLauncher .nds Maker
    • The latest release of HiyaCFW
    • The latest release of FWTool
    • A Nintendo DS NTR Catrtidge - (See picture below to tell if you have a Nintendo DS NTR Cartridge)

    Instructions

    1. Power off your device
    2. Insert your SD card into your computer
    3. Copy the contents of flipnotehax.zip to the root of your SD card
    4. Copy the contents of HiyaCFW.zip to the root of your SD card and rename HiyaCFW.nds to boot.nds.
    5. Copy the contents of FWTool.zip to the root of your SD card and rename FWTool to 2222.nds.
    6. Rename RocketLauncher.nds to 2222.nds and place the file in the root of your SD.
    7. Insert your NTR Cartridge.
    8. Power on your device and open Flipnote Studio.
    9. Find the folder named ugopwn and open it.
    10. Select the first flipnote and press Details
      11. , then Edit.
    11. "Copy" the first frame
    12. Exit the flipnote, and proceed to open and edit the second one.
    13. On the second flipnote, in the frame menu, press the right button on your DSi (if right handed) or the A button (if left handed)
    14. Tap the Paste button EXACTLY 122 TIMES.
    15. Press the Erase button, followed by the Paste button.
    16. Choose the options to dump nand_dsi.bin and CID.bin
    17. Power off your device.
    18. Copy nand_dsi.bin and CID.bin to your PC
    19. Obtain the rom file of the NTR cart you have. NOTE: We do not condone piracy.
    20. Open RocketLauncherNDSMaker.exe
    21. Follow the on-screen instructions and put the resulting file(s) on your SD Card
    22. Rename RocketLauncher.nds to 2222.nds and choose replace.
    23. Power on your device and open Flipnote Studio.
    24. Find the folder named ugopwn and open it.
    25. Select the first flipnote and press Details
      26. , then Edit.
    26. "Copy" the first frame
    27. Exit the flipnote, and proceed to open and edit the second one.
    28. On the second flipnote, in the frame menu, press the right button on your DSi (if right handed) or the A button (if left handed)
    29. Tap the Paste button EXACTLY 122 TIMES.
    30. Press the Erase button, followed by the Paste button.
    31. You should now be running HiyaCFW! You must repeat the Flipnote process every time to start HiyaCFW.

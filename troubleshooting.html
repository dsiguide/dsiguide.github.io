    Ugopwn gives me a white screen when I try to use it!

    Instructions

    Section I - Formatting Your SD Card (Windows)
    1. Open up the 'Computer' window by pressing Windows key + E on your keyboard.
    2. Your SD card should be displayed in the 'Devices with Removable Storage' section. Right-click on your SD card to bring up the right-click menu options. Select Format.
    3. In the Format window make sure that the correct File System is selected. It should be set to FAT32
    4. Select start, and wait for it to finish.
    5. Copy the necessary files over again and try the exploit again.
    Section II - Disable Flipnote Calendar on Boot
    1. Power on your DSi and navigate to Flipnote Studio.
    2. Press the gear icon in the top right corner.
    3. Press right on the screen, and turn 'Start on Calendar' to OFF.
    4. Now, follow the steps to try to run the exploit.

    HiyaCFW gives me an error!

    If the error says "FAT INIT FAILED", please redownload the files and check your SD card for errors.

    1. It is unknown at this time what to do if you get a generic error message.
    2. If you do, it is recommended that you power down you DSi, delete all files, and start over again.

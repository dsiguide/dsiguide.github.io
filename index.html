    What is homebrew?

    Homebrew means software that is not made or authorized by Nintendo. This lets you play games made by homebrew developers, edit your saves, and much, much more.

    DSi Homebrew is 100% Free! As long as you have the required materials, you're good to go.

    What is Custom Firmware?

    Custom Firmware (“CFW”) enables you to use more advanced hacks that any normal DSiWare homebrew can't acheive, like installing DSiWare backups, custom DSiWare apps, and anything else that only the ARM7 processor can do.

    CFW can be easily set up on any console.

    What does this guide install?

    This guide installs Unlaunch and a version of DSi Custom Firmware known as "HiyaCFW".

    What can I do with Custom Firmware?

    • Play all DSiWare games, without region locking!
    • Use “ROM hacks” for DSiWare games
    • Manage saves for many DSiWare games
    • Play games for older systems by using emulators (Unlaunch not required)
    • Install homebrew titles to your system, and have them appear on your Nintendo DSi Menu
    • Run many old Nintendo DS flash carts that were blocked long ago or never worked on the DSi/DSi XL

    What do I need to know before starting?

    • You should know that there is a chance your console could brick (not function at all) if you do something incorrectly. The chance of this is ALMOST 0% IF you follow instructions.
    • This guide will work on DSi and DSi XL consoles in all regions except China and Korea on firmware 1.4.5 or below. NOTE: THIS WILL NOT WORK ON ORIGINAL DS PHAT OR DS LITE!
    • Keep your DSi plugged in AT ALL TIMES DURING THIS PROCESS to avoid sudden power loss!
